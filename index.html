About Us

My name is Nuncia Alcantara. I have been a mother and a grandmother for many years, and taking care of my little ones has always been a big part of my life. Making snacks is one of my favorite activities because it gives me joy and keeps me busy. As an older person like me, I may not have as many things to do as before, but spending time in the kitchen makes my days more meaningful. I love preparing simple treats and trying new recipes that I know my grandchildren will enjoy. Seeing the little kids smile and happily eat the snacks I make truly warms my heart and brings me so much happiness. It is a simple pleasure, but it makes me feel fulfilled and loved.
